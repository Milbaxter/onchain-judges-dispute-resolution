The Pain Point: The "Justice Gap"

The digital economy is stuck between two extremes:

  • Traditional Courts: Too slow and expensive for a $500 dispute.
  • Smart Contracts: Blind. Code can calculate math, but it can't judge if a logo design is "good" or "bad."

The Solution: A multi-model arbitration layer. We aggregate the judgment of top-tier LLMs (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, Perplexity, Grok) to resolve disputes instantly.

Use Case 1: Freelance Marketplaces

The Problem: "Scope Creep" and subjective disputes.

Example: A client refuses to pay a developer, claiming the work is "buggy."

Without Polycourt: Funds are frozen for weeks while non-technical support agents guess who is right.

With Polycourt: The AI judges review the code against the contract requirements.

Verdict: "Code passes all test cases defined in the agreement. Funds released to freelancer."

Use Case 2: Crypto DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations)

The Problem: The "Oracle Gap."

Example: A DAO votes to pay a user for writing 5 marketing articles.

Without Polycourt: A human must manually verify the work and sign the transaction (slow, centralized).

With Polycourt: The user submits the article links. The AI verifies the content quality and topic accuracy.

Verdict: "Requirements met." The verdict is signed, and the smart contract automatically pays the user.

Use Case 3: Betting & Prediction Apps

The Problem: Edge cases and technical glitches.

Example: A server crash ends a video game match early.

Without Polycourt: Users get angry when the bet is marked as a "Loss."

With Polycourt: The AI reviews the game logs and the "House Rules."

Verdict: "Force Majeure event. Bet voided and refunded."

The "Secret Weapon": Privacy via TEEs

Most crypto projects force you to make everything public. Real businesses can't do that—they have NDAs and trade secrets.

How we solve this with TEEs (Trusted Execution Environments):

We run the AI models inside Oasis ROFL, which uses TEEs. Think of a TEE as a cryptographic "Black Box."

  1. Input: You upload your private contract and dispute evidence. It is encrypted immediately.
  2. Processing: The data enters the "Black Box." The AI judges read it inside the box to make a decision. Even the node operator cannot see what is happening inside.
  3. Output: The AI destroys the raw data and sends out only the verdict (e.g., "Alice wins").

Result: You get the transparency of blockchain enforcement without leaking your private data to the public.

Why is this connected to Crypto?

  1. Trustless Verification: You receive a digital signature proving that these specific AI models reached this consensus. You don't have to trust our company; you can verify the proof.
  2. Automated Enforcement: Because the verdict is on-chain, it can trigger a smart contract to move money immediately. No human middleman is needed to "press the button."
